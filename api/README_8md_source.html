30 ## What is BioDynaMo?
31 
32 BioDynaMo is a software platform to easily create, run, and visualise 3D agent-based biological simulations.
33 * **Blazing fast:** The core of the platform is writen in C++ and is highly optimized to harness the computational power of modern hardware.
34 * **Modular:** Reuse, adapt, or create modules that represents a specific biological behavior or entity. With BioDynaMo you don't need to start from scratch anymore!
35 * **Visual:** Effortlessly create astonishing images and animations of your simulations. Your publications and presentations will stand out even more!

52 ## Contributing
