   z = 0
   +-----+----+-----+
   | BW  | B  | BE  |
   +-----+----+-----+
   | W   | C  | E   |
   +-----+----+-----+
   | FW  | F  | FE  |
   +-----+----+-----+

   z = -1
   +-----+----+-----+
